Join Tozen Union

Tozen Union is run by its members. We welcome all workers in Japan who are willing to fight for fairer conditions. Here's how to join us.

  1. First, please complete the form below

  2. We'll contact you to discuss your application (if you have not already spoken with somebody from the union)

  3. Finally, we'll set up your union dues and approve your membership

If you have any difficulties or questions, please email field@tozenunion.org
